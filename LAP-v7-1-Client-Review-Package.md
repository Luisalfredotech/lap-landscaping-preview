### Image alt text
- Every `<img>` carries descriptive alt text combining subject + service + locale (e.g. *"Smart sprinkler and irrigation system over green lawn · Lake Norman, NC"*).

- Six dedicated service pages, all reachable from the home services grid and the footer Services column.

- Home anchors (`#services`, `#projects`, `#about`, `#reviews`, `#faq`, `#contact`) keep the homepage useful as a single-page conversion experience while still feeding the multi-page architecture.
