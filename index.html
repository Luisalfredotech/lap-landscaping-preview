The only people who will work on your landscape are LAP Landscaping employees. Our team members have experience and we will all be informed with your landscaping needs.

We can do all kinds of landscaping — tree and shrub pruning & installations, mulch, top soil, retaining walls, paver patios and walkways, sprinkler systems, sod installation, landscape lighting, lawn aeration, final grading, drainage systems, and much more.
