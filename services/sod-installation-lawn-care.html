Sod Installation & Lawn Care That Roots Right

A new lawn starts under the surface. We do the boring-but-critical work — final grading, soil prep, drainage correction — then lay fresh sod that roots evenly and looks finished from day one. Aeration, overseeding and seasonal care available too.

Request a free estimate
What's included
  • Final grading & soil preparation
  • Fresh sod installation (Bermuda, Fescue, Zoysia)
  • Lawn aeration & overseeding
  • Mulch installation & bed refresh
  • Tree & shrub pruning
  • Top soil & fertilization recommendations
Problems we solve
  • Patchy, weedy or thinning lawn
  • Bare areas after construction or tree removal
  • Uneven yard with low spots and mowing issues
  • Compacted soil that won't grow grass
Our process

A simple, four-step path from idea to finished yard.

  1. 01

    Consult

    We visit your property, listen to how you want to use the space and take measurements.

  2. 02

    Design

    You get a clear plan and a written quote — materials, layout, drainage, timing, and price.

  3. 03

    Build

    Our in-house crew prepares the site, installs hardscape and softscape, and cleans up daily.

  4. 04

    Maintain

    Optional seasonal care — aeration, mulch refresh, irrigation tuning — keeps it looking new.
