What's included
  • Florida tumbled travertine pool decks & patios
  • Marble and natural stone outdoor walkways
  • Mortar-set installation on proper base
  • Grouting and sealing for weather & pool-chemical resistance
  • Cutting and fitting around pools, curves and existing structures
  • Replacement and repair of existing stone surfaces
Problems we solve
  • Slippery, hot or deteriorating pool deck surface
  • Cracked or sunken pavers around the pool
  • Outdoor surface that can't handle heat, pool water and foot traffic
  • Want a natural stone look without the maintenance of unsealed material
Our process

A simple, four-step path from idea to finished yard.

  1. 01

    Consult

    We visit your property, listen to how you want to use the space and take measurements.

  2. 02

    Design

    You get a clear plan and a written quote — materials, layout, drainage, timing, and price.

  3. 03

    Build

    Our in-house crew prepares the site, installs hardscape and softscape, and cleans up daily.

  4. 04

    Maintain

    Optional seasonal care — aeration, mulch refresh, irrigation tuning — keeps it looking new.
