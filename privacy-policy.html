LAP Landscaping ("we," "us," "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard personal information when you visit our website, request an estimate, schedule services, or contact us in Troutman, Mooresville, Statesville, and the Lake Norman, NC area.

Information We Collect

When you request an estimate, schedule services, or contact us, we may collect:

  • Contact info: name, phone number, email address
  • Property & project details: service address, project scope, photos, preferred dates and times
  • Communication records: messages and call details related to quotes, scheduling, updates, and support
  • Website analytics: basic data such as pages visited and device/browser info via cookies or analytics tools

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide free estimates and respond to inquiries
  • Schedule appointments and send appointment confirmations or reminders
  • Deliver customer support and answer project questions
  • Send service updates related to active jobs
  • Improve our services and website experience

Text Messaging (SMS)

If you provide SMS consent or contact us by text, we may send text messages about quotes, scheduling, appointment confirmations, service updates, and customer support.

  • Message frequency varies based on your requests and service activity
  • Message and data rates may apply
  • Reply STOP at any time to unsubscribe. You may receive one final confirmation message.
  • Reply HELP for help, or contact us at 704-327-5663 or service@laplandscaping.com

See our full SMS Terms for additional details.

How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your mobile information, text messaging opt-in data, or messaging consent with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.

All the above categories exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent. This information will not be shared, sold, rented, or disclosed to any third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.

Your Choices

  • Access or deletion: contact us at 704-327-5663 or service@laplandscaping.com to request access to or deletion of your personal information
  • SMS opt-out: reply STOP to any text message from us
  • Questions: call 704-327-5663 or email service@laplandscaping.com
